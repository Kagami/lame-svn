Contact Information

MP3 encoder mailing list

For general questions, comments and bug reports about LAME and MP3 encoding, you can check the MP3 encoder mailing list.

Archives are also available (older archives: October 2000 - Sept 2001 and before October 2000 )

LAME developement mailing list

A mailing list related to Lame developement is available. It is mainly for developers discussions. Archives are provided by Sourceforge.

CVS mailing list

If you want to be automatically notified of commits to the CVS, you can subscribe to this list.

Bug tracker

To submit bug reports, you can use the bug tracker. Please be descriptive in your bug report. You should clearly mention your problem, LAME version used and how to reproduce the bug.
